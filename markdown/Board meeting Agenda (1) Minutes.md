**Agenda Item No.:-6 Minutes of Hearing Committee for various Ayurveda/Unani College under section 28/29 of NCISM Act, 2020 for the academic session 2026-27-regarding.**

It is submitted that the Ayurved/Unani colleges had been assessed on the basis of concerned regulations for the academic session 2026-27. On examination, it had appeared that, some colleges were not fulfilling the requirements for grant of LOI/Conditional permission. Therefore, the colleges had been given an opportunity of hearing in principle of natural justice before the designated Hearing Committee as appointed by The President, Medical assessment and Rating Board, NCISM. The Hearing had been conducted virtually i.e., through video conferencing and the link, timing and other details had been communicated separately to the concerned colleges and Hearing Committee Members.

In view of the above the **02 Members** of hearing committee undertook the hearing of below mentioned Ayurved/Unani colleges in terms of concerned regulations under the supervision of President, MARB, NCISM and the Minutes of the observation of the Hearing Committee were placed before the President, MARBISM. The verification of documents submitted by colleges was placed before the Hearing committee. The details are as under: -
