# 8. Assessment

# 8.1 Observation by the visitors

# Observation on functionality of all OPD's. :

The Hospital has Separate Registration Counter for OPD and IPD. Screening OPD, Kayachiktsa, Panchakarma, Shalya, Shalakya (Netra), Shalakya (Mukha), Kaumarabhritya, Prasooti Tantra & Stree Roga and Visha Chikitsa along with Casualty. All the patients are registered in the Central OPD and the HMIS software. Online HMIS, Central OPD and Respective Departmental OPD registers were cross matched and verified for random patients. Random Verification of OPD records were done and found satisfactory. Vishachikitsa OPD was functional with Agadatantra consultant. The Athyayika OPD had all equipments with working oxygen supply through, movable O2 concentrator, Emergency medicines and first-aid kit were present. ECG machine was functional. One Ambulance was found with hospital name. The central dispensary or Pharmacy was stocked. The pharmacy has maintained IPD and OPD dispensing register, the Indenting was done with Indent book, the central stock is maintained manually and stocks are verified every month. Data available were cross verified with purchase indent and bills. The medicine dispensing register form the pharmacy was compared with that of OPD and past records were also randomly verified and found to be correct.

# Observation on functionality of all Hospital. :

The hospital is 100 bedded, with separate wards for male and female Patients. The beds are numbered. All the 100 beds are of general ward category and well ventilated. Panchakrma theatre is centrally located and has separate section for Male and Female. Panchakarma block has different rooms for Vamana, Virechana, Basti and Raktamokshana. All these rooms have common washroom. The OPD and IP data on different procedures are maintained in registers. Data was verified and random case record were verified with central OPD and IPD register.The Hospital has on call anaesthetist for all OT procedure. All the modern Doctors are on call visit to the hospital in need. Major OT is equipped with Instrument. The OT complex has separate Autoclave, scrubbing, dressing sections, all registers of fumigation, sterilisation, autoclaving, swab test reports, were found adequate. NICU is available with radiant warmer and there is Phototherapy instrument. Clinical Laboratory is functional with facility. Radiology department has a 100MA digital portable X Ray Machine. AREB licence of Xray section is available, However, TLD badge is not available. ECG machine is available in Casualty. Physiotherapy section was functional. No ICU is available in the hospital. Overall the hospital was found to be functional. NICU Register, Immunisation register, USG, Ambulance, Death Record and Intern Duty rosters not available.

# Observation on Conditions of Deparments. :

The College has presently departments of 1st, 2nd and 3rd Professional BAMS. All the departments were functional. Two batch students were found in the class rooms with ongoing lecturer, about other students we had been told that, one batch was on vacation and other batch was facing exams. Samhita department does not have a Language laboratory, but procurement is in progress. Kriya department has all the instruments as per the Part 1 filled same was counted and found correct. The New Instruments as per MESA&R 2024 is in progress. Dissection theatre is located in next to Rachana Museum and has the tables and storage as part 1 along with Embalming Machine. There were one cadaver in the dissection theatre with upper limb and thorax been dissected. Dravya guna department have museum with all charts, modules and laboratory facility. Rasa Shatra & Bhaishaya Kalpana department have museum and department library books with all chart, modules and specimens. RSBk has separate Rasa Shala for practical classes with well equipped analytical laboratory equipments. Roga Nidana & Vikrita Vignana department had charts, models in spacious museum with computers along with printers attached were found. Swastavrutha and Yoga department have spacious hall with yoga matts and with one yoga staff present. Agada tantra & Vidhi Vaidyaka department have museum with well equipped and staff were present. Students interaction was done by the visitor. The classroom were found well and good with facility of Audio Visual aids, no any grievance recorded from the students. Accession register of central library was verified and found to be correct. Library has seating capacity of 120 students and digital library has 12 computers with Internet facility, same was verified. Herbal garden is in college campus with 4110 Sqmtr of space with 251 species and 1156 plants were found. The Clinical Departments of the College Kayachiktsa, Panchakarma, Prassoti Stree Roga, Kaumarabhritya, Shalya and Shalakya Tantra have been established with necessary charts, models and books as per the requirement. The Boys hostel and the girls hostel is located near to the college within the campus. CCTV monitoring system is adequate. The mess is located in the college campus with adequate seating capacity.

Overall both the college and Hospital is functional.The College has 52 full time and 2 Part time Faculties (1 Biostatistician and 1 Yoga teacher). Aadhar Based Biometric Attendance (Thumb) has been Started from 1st April for teaching Staff and Iris based from 12/04/2025. The Simultation models are present in clinical departments. Majority of the College Teaching staff are residing in the staff quarters provided by the college.
